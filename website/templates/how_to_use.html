Use it judiciously to minimize its environmental impact.

  • After use, ensure the syringe cap is securely placed.
  • If there is any unused product left in the syringe, it is advisable to use it in other areas or keep it for future use to reduce waste.
  • Put the sealed syringe in a plastic bag, close it tightly, and dispose of it in your regular trash, carefully separating it from recyclables.
  • Be aware of the local legislation for the disposal of certain products in some areas.
  • Do not empty the syringe or its contents into the toilet, drain, or sink; this helps to avoid any environmental impact and ensure proper waste management.
  • Keep any unused leftover syringes in a safe place out of the reach of children and pets, and follow any additional storage recommendations provided on the product packaging.
